ABOUT US



Aim

Our Aim is to provide easy and reliable solutions through robotics for the problems we encounter in our daily life

Mission

At its heart robotics is about using science & engineering to find creative & practical solution to ease the difficulty faced by humans while performing various tasks in majority of sectors. We aim to develope and fabricate manual & autonomous robots; advanced drones and inovative system utilizing advanced control systems combined with sophisticated matchine design for reliable, efficient and simple solution.

Values

At NEXTBOTS ROBOTICS we have a supportive work environment with colaboration from a diverse group of practically inclined engineers with a firm theoretical background. We share common passion for the field of robotics. With a ‘never give up! ‘ attitude in a collaborative environment we think, inovate, experiment to bring out the best of existing techonology and implement in our product.
